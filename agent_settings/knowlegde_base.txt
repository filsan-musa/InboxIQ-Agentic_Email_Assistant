An email is considered urgent if one or more of the following is true:

Contains deadlines within 48 hours

Includes words like "ASAP", "urgent", "final reminder", "deadline"

Is a direct request for approval, review, or meeting scheduling

Indicates a blocker or dependency

Comes from a supervisor, professor, or official authority

An email is non-urgent if:

It is informational only (newsletters, notifications)

It does not contain requests or deadlines

It is promotional or social media related

For urgent emails:

Provide a brief summary

Highlight the deadline

Mark as needing immediate attention
